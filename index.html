Do you need a hassle-free way to monitor product temperature in refrigerators, freezers and coolers? Forget about having to check the temperature yourself--our automated temperature monitoring systems protect your valuable product 24/7. Data loggers also ensure regulatory compliance, send alarm notifications on change in temp or power outage, and provide you with peace of mind

Vaisala fridge and freezer monitoring, alarming, and reporting solutions reduce the risk of lost product and regulatory non-compliance for your life science environments and critical cold storage applications. Ideal for remote freezer temperature monitoring, and with multiple connectivity options wired and wireless, the system is simple to set up and easy to use.

Usually one sensor per piece of hardware is required, however in larger devices or spaces, more than one sensor may be desirable. The number of sensors supported by TempGenius is unlimited. Multiple receivers and signal repeaters can accommodate coverage over very large areas, multiple floors and neighboring buildings. The TempGenius team can arrive at conservative coverage with background on your space.
